    OUR PANEL RANGE

    DG & Automatic Synchronising Panels

    JCI has Expertise in Double Busbar systems with several DG sets connected to DG bus and selected DG sets continuously running for critical loads.

    Power Control Centre (PCC) panels

    We are one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of Power Control Centre (PCC) panels in Bangalore and other parts of Karnataka, that are used for diverse industrial applications.

    MCC (Motor Control Centre) Panels

    We offer motor control center panels in both the single front and double front designs.

    APFC panels (Automatic Power Factor Correction Panels)

    We offer APFC panels (Automatic Power Factor Correction Panels)  with Capacitor Banks with ample space provided for the Capacitors within the panels for efficient heat dissipation.

    Fire Fighting Panels

    The offered firefighting panel is highly liked for fire-preventive, auto-operation in the event of pressure drop. The offered firefighting panel is available in wall-mounted and floor-mounted configurations.

    AHU / Metering Panels

    We supply high-quality AHU and Metering panels. Our AHU panel is equipped with the facility for remote operations It becomes compatible with BMS through potential-free contacts.

    PLC & Relay Control Panels

    Being one of the leading manufacturers of PLC panels, our programmable logic controller panels are specially designed and engineered to accommodate the analog and digital components and circuits.

    Control Desks / Customised Power Distribution Boards

    Our range of Industrial Control Desks is highly valued for its durable construction and excellent quality. Our Industrial Control Desks are available in varied dimensions and specifications as per the client’s requirement.

    UPS distribution Boards

    We supply UPS distribution boards to industrial establishments. With careful analysis and consideration of electrical loads and points, one can trust JCI for the successful installation and commissioning of UPS panel boards.

    Main Lighting Panels / Chiller Panels

    Our supplied main lighting panel is suitable for both commercial and domestic applications. The offered main lighting panel is available at a very affordable market price.

    Outdoor / Weatherproof Panels

    We design special outdoor panel boards to withstand harsh and extreme weather conditions. The panels are durable, efficient and offer a long functional life after installation.
